In Details

  • Kavach is a easy to use and portable UV sterilizer which is helping the world in this Fight against coronavirus. It kills viruses, bacteria, and other disease-causing pathogens that may be present on day to day products.
  • The design of Kavach is made such that it provides 360-degree thorough disinfection of the objects. It has been made in accordance with a study published by Nebraska Institute of Bio-medical research and trusted by doctors, common people..
  • The objects with are placed inside depending on the quantity can be disinfected in time ranging merely from 2 mins to 10 mins. Instead of keeping the groceries out in the sun for 12 hours and hoping the virus gets killed- using Kavach to sterilize the items is a care-free and sure-shot way.
  • Kavach comes in two design requirements for hospitals and household purpose
  • Spacious design; Removable mesh for easy cleaning; 360-degree sterilization; No chemicals; Increase in shelf life of fruits and vegetables.

Working

  • Place the objects which are to be sanitized in the box with sufficient spacing
  • Close the door and on the switch for 10 mins
  • Your items are sterilized and safe for you to use.
UV light- The UV light wavelength used is 256.3 nm.
Standard in all the medical equipment and known for its high germicidal property.
